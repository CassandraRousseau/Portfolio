About

Hi and welcome to my website! I am an interdisciplinary artist practicing graphic and performing arts. I have a diploma in Visual Arts and soon graduating from a Specialization in Computation Arts and a minor in Theatre.

I am currently a web designer at the Canadian ministery Canada Economic Development for Quebec Regions.

Outside of my studies, I am also a member of the Latin and heels dance crew Fierce MTL. I have a training in Hip-hop and was previously in K-pop dance crews called Kollective and D-Krown.

My work focuses mostly on exploration and storytelling. I love to combine various elements from different practices to create unique and authentic pieces.I have a strong an interest in immersive experiences. My goal is to push the limits of immersive technologies by exploring the merge between traditional or performative disciplines with digital tools and techniques.

Apart from my academic and professional background, I have a bubbly and energetic personality but also very hardworking and curious to learn new subjects.

I have interests in tons of subjects: tech, arts, entertainment, politics, and so much more.
