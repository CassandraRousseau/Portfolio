Corps en mouvement (2019)

For this project on the theme of the human being, I decided to use dance as my subject. Dance is an important part of my life, and I wanted to include it in this project. What's more, I felt that this artistic domain is one of the best themes for showcasing the human being in all his grace and splendor. It brings together both bodily and emotional expression. In this series, I wanted to represent one of my passions and depict people who are part of my daily life. I tried to realize this part of my daily life by creating these images. To emphasize the body's movements, I decided to shoot in black and white, so that the colors wouldn't detract from them. The contrast between black and white highlights the bodies and their movements. The images are mostly blurred, as I felt this effect accentuated the body's movements and fluidity to the maximum. What's more, this imprecision adds speed, power, emotion and dynamism to the series. Several camera angles and shot types were used in this project to show the variety of movements. In this series, I've integrated group and individual photos to show the variety of performances and the power that each can achieve.The aim of this project is to highlight the power that our bodies can exude through gestures, emotions and expressions alone.I want to show how our bodies can create an experience, an atmosphere, a story all on their own.I wanted to express how dance can help us discover and experience emotions, an adventure, a universe for a moment.
Series made with a digital camera and Photoshop.
Dimensions: 3x(7200px x 2400px).
